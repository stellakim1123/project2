Hawaiian Adventure

Adventure paradise

Hawaii’s beautiful beaches are just the beginning. From surfing the gentle waves of Waikiki Beach to snorkeling with manta rays on the Kona Coast, every moment in Hawaii is filled with fun. From adrenaline-pumping adventures to activities for the whole family, Hawaii is the place to go for adventure.

Discover the adventure

Surfing

If you dream of carving up waves, there's no better place than Hawaii, the birthplace of surfing.

Snorkeling and scuba diving

Explore Hawaii's underwater world, surrounded by approximately 1,931 km of coral reefs, and come face-to-face with tropical fish, spotted rays, and green sea turtles.

Mountain Tubing

Strap on a headlamp and hop into a tube to explore inland canals, tunnels and flumes on this only-in-Kauai adventure.

Parasailing

Lift off on a parasailing adventure and get a bird’s eye view of Hawaii’s breathtaking coastline from up to 1,200 feet in the air.

Deep Sea Fishing

Hawaii is home to some of the biggest marlin in the world as well as one of the oldest billfish tournaments, plus scores of other game fish – from wahoo to mahimahi to giant trevally.

Whale watching

During the winter, Hawaii becomes the world's premier whale watching destination, as thousands of kohola(humpback whales) come to feed and raise their young. Take advantage of tours available on every island to see these magnificent creatures up close.

Inland Adventures

  • Zipline

    Hop on one of the longest ziplines in the U.S. and fly through Hawaii's lush forests, taking in some of the most beautiful secnery in the world.

  • Lapel

    Whether you're rappelling down jungle cliffs or cascading waterfalls, Hawaii's expert juides will show you the ropes.

  • ATV Tours

    Looking for something a little more adventures? Take an ATV ride to explore rugged terrain or hard-to-reach areas of the Hawaiian Islands.
